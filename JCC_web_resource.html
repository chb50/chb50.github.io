Beginner Guides and Tools

Tae Kim's Guide to Japanese: link>>

Tae Kim's Guide to Japanese is a great starting point for getting into the japanese language. It teaches you the most basic grammar and vocab that you will need to know for everyday conversation by having seperate lessons and practice for each grammar point and vocab list

Rikaichan/Rikaikun web browser addons

Rikaichan for Firefox: link>>

Rikaikun for Chrome: link>>

Rikaichan/Rikaikun is a great tool for when you are browsing sites in Japanese. this addon, when enabled, will highlight any Japanese word that you hover your mouse over and it will give you both its reading and its definition instantly.
